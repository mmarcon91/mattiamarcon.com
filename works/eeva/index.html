Eeva
Electronics
12 • 05 • 14
design with
Gregorio Aiello
Alessio Buccino
Massimo De Marchi

The aim of this robotic toy, EEVA, is to interact with children both in an autonomous mode, with a funny and particular personality given by its peculiar sounds and multicolor lights, both in a controlled mode, using an iPhone/iPad as remote. Moreover the kid will be able to wake EEVA up and make it sleep by just knocking on its head and covering the top of its case, respectively. The kid could also build some paths of different difficulty and try to drive EEVA along them. More kids could challenge on the same path using their EEVA.
