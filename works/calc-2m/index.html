Calc 2M is a simple and easy to use calculator that can solve expressions and share the results.

With its well designed and easy to use keyboard, Calc 2M allows you to insert complex expression and export those everywhere and with built in sharing functions you can easily share your results with your friends !

Moreover now, with the built-in copy and paste feature, you can easily copy an expression from everywhere and paste it in the app display !
